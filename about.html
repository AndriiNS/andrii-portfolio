Then I started learning front-end and in 1-2 years I was already making websites for my friends and acquaintances. Now I work for a company that deals with business school and make advertising landing pages for them, you can see them in my projects. But I work part-time, so my salary is low. Now I am looking for a professional company where I can develop.

=>

Feedback is very important to me, because I can't always see my mistakes, and when there is a team or people checking the result, it's much easier to work. I don't send the result to the client if I don't like it. All my work was closed with minimal edits. But once I had a client who didn't know what he wanted. He asked me to redesign the same block several times, and he didn't like it. It wasn't a designer's mistake or my mistake, but the client wanted this block to have a completely different design. So I agreed with him that I would charge for each additional hour of work and soon finished this project
